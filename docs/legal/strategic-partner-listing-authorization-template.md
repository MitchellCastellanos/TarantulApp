## LISTING DISPLAY AUTHORIZATION AGREEMENT

**Effective date:** [DATE]

**Between**

1. **[TARANTULAPP LEGAL ENTITY NAME]**, with principal address at [ADDRESS] (“**TarantulApp**”),

and

2. **[VENDOR LEGAL BUSINESS NAME]**, with principal address at [ADDRESS] (“**Partner**”).

### 1. Purpose

Partner participates in TarantulApp’s **invite-only founding strategic partner** program. TarantulApp operates a hobby-focused application and marketplace. Partner wishes certain **product listing information** to appear in TarantulApp so that users can **discover** Partner’s offers and **navigate to Partner’s own website or sales channels** to complete transactions.

### 2. Grant of license (display only)

Subject to this Agreement, Partner grants TarantulApp a **non-exclusive, revocable, worldwide, royalty-free** license during the Term to:

- **Reproduce, transmit, store, format, and display** on TarantulApp (including websites and mobile apps) the **Listing Content** (defined below) for **discovery and comparison** in the marketplace experience;
- **Create thumbnails or resized renditions** of product images where technically necessary;
- **Apply labels or badges** (e.g. “Official partner”, “Certified partner”) that **identify Partner’s listings** as distinct from **community / peer-to-peer** listings;
- **Normalize** Listing Content (e.g. species names, currency display, stock indicators) **without altering** Partner’s underlying commercial meaning.

**“Listing Content”** means product information Partner makes available for this purpose, including without limitation: title, description (if provided), price, currency, availability/stock indicators, product image URLs, species or product identifiers, shipping or region text where applicable, and **canonical URLs** to Partner’s product pages or storefront.

### 3. What TarantulApp does *not* do (unless separately agreed)

Unless the parties execute a **separate written agreement**, TarantulApp **does not** become the merchant of record, **does not** guarantee fulfillment, and **does not** process Partner’s sales. **Purchases occur between the end user and Partner** (or Partner’s platforms). TarantulApp may surface **Partner’s official links** and contact paths.

### 4. Accuracy and updates

Partner remains responsible for the **accuracy** of Listing Content. TarantulApp may refresh Listing Content **periodically** using methods agreed between the parties (e.g. **API, CSV/feed export, or other documented technical means**). Partner will use **commercially reasonable efforts** to notify TarantulApp of material errors or restrictions.

### 5. Intellectual property

Partner represents it has the rights necessary to grant the license in Section 2 for the Listing Content. Partner’s **name, marks, and logos** may be displayed **solely** to identify Partner and its listings. TarantulApp may not imply sponsorship beyond what is accurate.

### 6. Differentiation from community sellers

Partner acknowledges that TarantulApp may display **community / peer listings** separately from Partner’s listings. **Badges and ranking rules** may favor Partner listings **when consistent with TarantulApp’s product policies** and disclosed to users.

### 7. Term and termination

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues until **terminated**.

Either party may terminate:

- **For convenience** with **[30] days’** prior written notice; or  
- **Immediately** if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within **[10] days** of written notice (if curable).

Upon termination, TarantulApp will **cease new publication** of Partner’s Listing Content within a **reasonable period** and remove or disable Partner’s listings **within [14] days**, except where retention is required by law.

### 8. Confidentiality (lightweight)

“**Confidential Information**” means non-public business information disclosed in connection with this program. The receiving party will use the same degree of care it uses for its own similar information, but **no less than reasonable care**, and will not disclose Confidential Information except to advisors bound by confidentiality or as required by law.

### 9. Disclaimer of warranties / limitation of liability

**To the maximum extent permitted by law**, Listing Content is provided **“as is”** and TarantulApp **disclaims warranties** not expressly stated. **To the maximum extent permitted by law**, each party’s **aggregate liability** under this Agreement for **direct damages** will not exceed **[USD/CAD $____]** except for **fraud, willful misconduct, gross negligence,** or **breach of Section 8** (Confidentiality).
